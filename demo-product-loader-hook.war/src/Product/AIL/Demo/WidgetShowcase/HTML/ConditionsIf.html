In this example the "if" widget is used to enabled or disable a parsedUrlContent widget.

Note that this condition is applied when the page is loaded. Use the Submit Page button at the foot of the page to force the page to reload.

PageFlow 
<question title="Display conditional 'if' section" binding="/asset[id='conditionsData']/attribute[id='if']" />
<if condition="i:test(/asset[id='conditionsData']/attribute[id='if']/value[.='Yes'])">
    <parsedUrlContent url="~/HTML/ConditionMet.html"/>
</if>
Model 
<asset id="conditionsData" assetTypeId="ConditionsData">
    <attribute id="if" value="No" format="yesorno" />
</asset>
