A page element to display a summary of a quotation or policy that contents information that is useful to a broker.

This widget can be used from PageFlows, but it also designed to be used to define the body text of broker notification emails. The email generator is pre-configured to use this template to generate the body text. The template can of course be overriden within the product hierarchy to provide different templates for each product, or for product suites.

More information on broker notifications and how to use them can be found on the Configuring Email Notifications wiki page.
