Display summary information about an party's account and payment schedule.

The options listed below control what information is presented and in what format. Information is taken from the financial account and transaction data associated with a party on the policy and and a payment schedule held on the policy.

When an accountBinding is specified a summary of the account will be displayed including status, balance, opening date, currency etc. Additionally, if showStatement is set to true the a statement showing transactions on the account is also included. The period option controls how far back in history the statement should cover.

If showNormalizedSchedule is set to true then the payment scheduled identified by scheduleBinding is displayed in its normalized form. In this form a payment that is set to repeat monthly is listed as one entry.

The showActualizedSchedule options is similar, but repeating payments are shown "unrolled". So, if period is set to 3 Month, a repeating payment which is valid for the whole period would show as three separate payments. Also, in this mode, when multiple payments fall due on the same day they are rolled up into one row with a description explaining how they are made up.

Pageflow 
    <accountDetails [title="title"]
                       [accountBinding="binding"]
                       [scheduleBinding="binding"]
                       [period="period"] 
                       [showNormalizedSchedule="true/false"]
                       [showActualizedSchedule="true/false"]
                       [showStatement="true/false"]
                       [renderHint="render hint"] />
  • title Title text for the base question.
  • accountBinding XPath pointing to the party in the current policy for whom an account summary should be rendered.
  • showStatement Include a statement for the selected account.
  • scheduleBinding XPath pointing to a payment schedule in the policy to display payments for.
  • period Defines the period for the statement and payment schedules in ISO 8601 format.
  • showNormalizedSchedule Render the payment schedule in normalized format.
  • showActualizedSchedule Render the payment schedule in actualized format..
  • renderHint Render hint to be output.
Examples

Display details for the broker's account including a statement covering the last 3 Months.

<accountDetails title="Broker's account" accountBinding="/broker" showStatement="true" period="P3M" />

Display the client's account with a statement covering the last 6 Months.

<accountDetails title="Client's account statement" accountBinding="/client" showStatement="true" period="P6M" />

Show an actualized payment scheduled from the policy's payment details covering the next 3 months.

<accountDetails title="Client's actualized payment schedule" scheduleBinding="/paymentDetails"" showActualizedSchedule="true" period="P3M" />

Show an normalized payment scheduled from the policy's payment details covering the next 3 months.

<accountDetails title="Client's normalized payment schedule" scheduleBinding="/"paymentDetails"" showNormalizedSchedule="true" period="P3M" />

Display the client's account summary and statement for the previous year and the normalized payment schedule for the coming year.

<accountDetails title="Client's normalized payment schedule" accountBinding="/client" showStatement="true" scheduleBinding="/"paymentDetails"" showNormalizedSchedule="true" period="P1Y" />
