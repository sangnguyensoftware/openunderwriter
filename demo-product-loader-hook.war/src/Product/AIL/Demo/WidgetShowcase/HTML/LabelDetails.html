Display/update the labels associated with an object.

Pageflow
    <labelDetails [title="title"]
                     [binding="target object"]
                     [discriminator="list of discriminators"]
                     [readOnly="true|false"]
                     [addAndDeleteEnabled="true|false"]
                     [renderHint="render hint"] />
  • title Title text for the page element.
  • binding
  • discriminator A comma separated list of discriminators which may be used to restrict the available labels.
  • readOnly
  • addAndDeleteEnabled
  • renderHint Render hint to be output.
